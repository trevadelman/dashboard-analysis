What is this app?

A self-hosted trading dashboard that connects to your Alpaca brokerage account. It has two modes that work independently or together:

  • Manual mode — you use the Analysis Dashboard and Scanner to find setups, then place orders yourself from the Positions page.
  • Autonomous mode — the bot scans the market on a schedule, finds signals, and places bracket orders automatically.

Pages at a glance

Analysis DashboardChart + indicators for a single symbol. Enter orders manually.
Market ScannerBatch scan a universe of symbols. Load cached results or run a fresh scan.
Market PulseBroad market regime — SPY, QQQ, sector ETFs, breadth indicators.
PositionsLive open positions, P&L, and order management.
BacktestRun the momentum strategy against historical data for any symbol.

First-time setup

  • Open Settings → Alpaca and add your API key + secret. Use paper trading to start.
  • Optionally configure an AI provider (Ollama or DeepSeek) in Settings → AI for AI-assisted signal scoring.
  • Set your risk % and max positions in Settings → App.

How the scan works

Every symbol is evaluated in two tiers. A symbol must pass Tier 1 before Tier 2 is run.

Tier 1 — RegimeIs the stock in a bullish or bearish trend? Uses EMA stack (9/21/50) and price structure. Bearish stocks are excluded from BUY signals.
Tier 2 — Setup qualityCompression (Bollinger Band width percentile), Relative Strength vs SPY, and Relative Volume. All three must meet thresholds for a signal.

Universes

All Sectors (~500)Static curated list of ~500 liquid GICS stocks. Always available, no cache needed.
Full UniverseCached Alpaca equity universe (~2,000+ symbols). Requires a "Refresh from Alpaca" first. Equity only — no crypto.
All Crypto (Alpaca)All USD-quoted crypto pairs tradeable on Alpaca (~25 pairs).
Custom ListComma-separated symbols you type in. Useful for watchlists.

Result columns

RegimeBULLISH / BEARISH / NEUTRAL based on EMA stack.
TierHow far the symbol got (1 = regime only, 2 = full setup scored).
SignalBUY, SELL, or NONE. Only Tier 2 symbols get a signal.
Score / Grade0–100 composite score. A = 80+, B = 65+, C = 50+, D = below 50.
Rel StrengthPrice return vs SPY over the lookback period. Positive = outperforming.
RVOLRelative volume — today's volume vs the 20-day average. >1.5 = elevated.
BB Width %Bollinger Band width as a % of price. Low = compressed = potential breakout.

Cache

Every completed scan is saved to a local cache. The autonomous bot also writes to this cache on its scheduled runs. Click Load Cache to view the latest results without re-scanning.

Text filter

Type in the filter box to instantly narrow results. Matches against symbol, regime, signal, and grade. Examples: BUY, BULLISH, NVDA, A.

Symbol lookup

Type any ticker in the search box. The dashboard fetches OHLCV bars and runs the full indicator suite. Crypto symbols use slash format (e.g. BTC/USD).

Timeframes

Long (Daily)1 year of daily bars. Best for swing-to-position trades held days to weeks.
Swing (Hourly)3 months of hourly bars. Best for multi-day swing trades.
Short (15-min)1 month of 15-minute bars. Best for intraday and short-term trades.

Indicator panels

Price + EMAsCandlestick chart with 9/21/50 EMA overlays. EMA stack alignment = trend direction.
RSI14-period RSI. Above 55 = bullish momentum, below 45 = bearish.
MACD12/26/9 MACD. Histogram color shows momentum direction.
Bollinger Bands20-period BB. Narrow bands = compression = potential breakout setup.
VolumeBar volume with 20-day average overlay. Spikes confirm moves.

Signal output

The signal card shows the Tier 1 + Tier 2 verdict, score, grade, suggested entry/stop/target, and risk-reward ratio. If AI is configured, an AI confidence score is also shown.

Placing orders

Use the Order panel to submit market or limit orders directly to Alpaca. Position size is calculated automatically based on your risk % setting and the suggested stop distance.

Decision flow

The bot runs on a fixed schedule. Every job follows this sequence:

  • Circuit breakers — if manually halted or daily loss limit breached, stop immediately.
  • Exit detection — compare current positions to last known; log any that disappeared (stop/target hit).
  • Position review — for each open position, run the reviewer and trail the stop or force-exit if warranted.
  • Entry scan — scan the watchlist; submit bracket orders for new signals that pass all gates.

Schedule (equity watchlists)

9:30 ET Mon–FriSnapshot opening equity, reset daily halt.
9:35 ET Mon–FriReview long (daily) positions.
9:40 ET Mon–FriScan for long signals.
Every 15 minScan for short (15-min) signals.
Every 60 minScan for swing signals + review swing positions.
Every 5 minExit detection poller.

Schedule (crypto watchlists)

Same interval jobs, but the three daily jobs fire at midnight ET (7 days/week) instead of 9:30 ET Mon–Fri. Crypto positions are reviewed 24/7.

Settings

Enable autonomousMaster switch. Off = bot scans but never places orders.
Scan WatchlistUniverse the bot scans. All Sectors = ~500 stocks. Full Universe = full cached Alpaca equity list.
Max Daily Loss %If account equity drops this % from the opening snapshot, the bot halts for the rest of the day.
Entry CooldownMinimum hours between bot entries on the same symbol. Prevents re-entering a position too soon.
Review TimeframesWhich timeframes the position reviewer runs on. Match to the timeframes you trade.

Orders

The bot always submits bracket orders — a limit entry with a stop loss and take profit baked in. If the bot goes offline, your stops remain active on Alpaca's servers.

Circuit breaker

Triggered automatically when the daily loss limit is breached, or manually via the Pause button on the dashboard. Resets at the next market open (or midnight for crypto).

Open positions

Shows all currently open positions from your Alpaca account. Refreshes automatically every 30 seconds.

Unrealized P&LCurrent mark-to-market gain/loss on the position.
Cost BasisAverage entry price × quantity.
Market ValueCurrent price × quantity.

Closing positions

Use the Close button to submit a market order to close the full position immediately. For partial closes, use the Analysis Dashboard order panel.

Orders

The Orders tab shows all open and recently filled orders. Bracket order legs (stop loss, take profit) appear as child orders under the parent entry order.

What it tests

Runs the same momentum strategy (Tier 1 + Tier 2) against historical OHLCV data for a single symbol. Simulates entries and exits using the same signal logic the live bot uses.

Parameters

SymbolAny equity or crypto symbol.
TimeframeLong (daily), Swing (hourly), or Short (15-min).
Start / End dateHistorical range to test over.
Initial capitalStarting account size for the simulation.
Risk %% of equity risked per trade (same as the live bot setting).

Results

Total returnNet % gain/loss over the test period.
Win rate% of trades that were profitable.
Avg RAverage return in units of initial risk (R). >1.0 = positive expectancy.
Max drawdownLargest peak-to-trough equity decline during the test.
Sharpe ratioRisk-adjusted return. >1.0 is generally considered acceptable.

Limitations

  • No slippage or commission modelling — live results will differ.
  • Signals are generated bar-by-bar with no lookahead, but fills assume the next bar's open price.
  • Only tests one symbol at a time — portfolio-level backtesting is not supported.
